About Us

What is After Image?
Content is everywhere more than ever. Sports, social media, tv, film, and vertical media are among a sea of products competing for our attention daily. Film is one of those mediums that requires the most attention and time. Because of this, social media around film has become focused primarily on ratings or how ”good” a piece of content is. With attention in limited supply, these ratings have become instrumental in sifting through everything to find something worth their time. Rotten Tomatoes is a crucial example of this, with the Tomatometer becoming a key indicator of a film's success. But this obsession with criticism has, in our opinion at After Image, proved detrimental to the viewing experience. Instead of enjoying a movie and discussing its themes, the audience's reaction is more attuned to the Rotten Tomatoes approach.

After Image is a social media discussion platform that has ten movies highlighted each month, each with different budgets, languages, mediums and time periods. Users can be part of a global film club to watch and discuss the same films each month. Its a place where advertising, Rotten Tomatoes and ratings matter less and where deciding what to watch is less tiresome.

Users can log into an account, create a profile, browse through different films and read the posts and discussion threads associated with each film. They can contribute to the discussion, create threads, and save/favorite posts they enjoy. Film criticism is still welcome but so is analysis or any sort of engagement.
Our Mission
Our purpose with After Image is, first and foremost, to foster an online community for genuine engagement and love for film. Different features will be proposed, created, removed and adapted to serve that goal.
