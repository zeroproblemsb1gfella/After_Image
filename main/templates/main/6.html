AFTERSUN

Directed by Charlotte Wells

Starring Paul Mescal as Calum and Frankie Corio as Sophie

Aftersun is a lovely film that serves both as a coming of age film for Sophie and a character study of Calum, a young divorced father trying to do best by his daughter Sophie. Its plot is centered around a vacation in Turkey the two took 15 years ago. The story is told with a combination of striking cinematography and old home video footage as Adult Sophie tries to piece together what happened to her father and why.

Oscar Nomination

Paul Mescal received an Oscar nomination for best actor in the 2023 Oscars. He is rapidly becoming a rising star with performances in the hit TV show Normal People and the 2021 film The Lost Daughter.

Charlotte Wells

Charlotte Wells is a Scottish filmmaker based in New York. She is a graduate of the MBA/MFA dual-degree program at NYU where she wrote and directed three short films and produced many more. Charlotte was a Fellow at the 2020 Sundance Institute Screenwriters and Directors Labs with her first feature Aftersun which premiered in Semaine de la Critique at Cannes in 2022.

Waterworks

Be warned that Aftersun is a brutally honest film that can be extremely sad for some. Its depiction of depression is nothing short of heartwrenching.
