pretend that you love me.
Joel Haver blends reality and fiction in his 2020 no budget film. It is a deeply personal film that explores his romantic relationships as he wrestles with his own health and the health of his father. Joel Haver is a filmmaker popular on YouTube who is responsible for a number of no budget films and garnered over 1 and a half million subscribers for his sketch comedy. He is a proponent of no budget filmmaking and holds a yearly "Movies That Were Made During The Oscars" event in which his followers make full feature length films in the 3 hour duration of the Oscars. Anti-establishment, inspiring and creative, Joel Haver is a maverick in the filmmaking world.
