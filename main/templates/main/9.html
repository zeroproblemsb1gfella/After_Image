Extended Synopsis:

The show was largely based on the true crime story and nonfiction book written by Jon Krakauer. Detective Jeb Pyre upon the investigation of a murder of a saint mother and her baby daughter that were involved in a church had caused his mind to whither. The story places a lot of emphasis on the themes portrayed by religiosity and family and how the mind of the detective comes to terms with the things that are keeping him afloat in his mind and the things that can drift away his sanity and what possible outcomes lay for him yet.
