Infinity Pool is a Horror Thriller set in the fictional resort island of La Tolqa. The couple James and Em Foster are on vacation, enjoying the all-inclusive beach resort when suddenly a horrible series of events leads to revealing a hidden sybaritic subculture within leading to unbridled brutality and dreamlike horrors.

The HORROR

Infinity Pool isn't your normal horror thriller. It doesn't play by the generic rules of regular horror films, it takes you into a dark void and really makes you think of the capabilities of humans. It drags you through the unsettling experience making you think what twisted images will appear next on screen. It allows you to really delve deep into the disturbing possibilities of human nature, told through an unconventional way. It breaks the normalities of storytelling and takes it's own unique route to unfold these horrors.
