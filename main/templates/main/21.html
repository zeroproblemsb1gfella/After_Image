Pickpocket

Directed By: Robert Bresson

Starring: Martin LaSalle as Michel | Marika Green as Jeanne | Jean Pélégri as L'inspecteur principal

Pickpocket is a French film that tells the story of Michel. A man who under his undesirable circumstances turns to pickpocketing in order to survive. As Michel hones his skills, he latches on unwanted police attention and dispite objections from his friends, family and potential lover he has a hard time resisting the urge to steal.

Masterpiece of French Cinema

Pickpocket is considered an old classic by many film critics. The style and theme of the film have often been used for influence and inspiration for many filmmakers such as Martin Scorsese, director of big name films such as The Wolf of Wall Street, Goodfellas, and The Departed.

The film brings a unique experience for the watcher as it has a minimalist style, to create the environment of realism. It brings the watcher straight into Michels complex fight with his moral compass as there are extremely hard ethical questions to be answered within the film.
